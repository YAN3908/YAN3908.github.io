A rather complex system due to the large number of levers on the boom, with friction units of different sizes and loads, which, accordingly, require different doses of lubricant, and distributors with 14-16 outlets had to be used to ensure the calculated volumes of lubricant. The system was installed in 2015, based on the station and monoblock distribution valves manufactured by GRACO (USA). The loader during this time worked on loading coal, potassium, loading phosphorites, loading wood chips and cleaning holds and warehouses. During this period, the wear of friction units was not recorded.
